What is Omega 3?

Omega-3 fatty acids are essential fats that your body needs but cannot produce on its own. These fats are important for heart health, brain function, and reducing inflammation. Fish oil is one of the best natural sources of omega-3s.

Its category is: Pre-Workout. Its purpose is to warm up muscles before training.

Benefits:

  • Supports heart health
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Improves brain function and eye health
  • Boosts mood and mental health

How to use Omega 3?

Follow the dosage instructions on the packaging or as advised by your healthcare provider. Typically, 1-2 tablets daily is common.
