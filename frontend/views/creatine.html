What is Creatine?

Creatine is a natural substance found in muscle cells that helps your skeletal muscles to produce energy during high-intensity exercise. Supplementing with creatine increases your muscles' phosphocreatine stores, allowing you to produce more ATP energy to fuel your muscles during high-intensity workouts.

Its category is: Post-Workout.

Benefits:

  • Increases muscle mass and strength
  • Improves exercise performance and recovery
  • Supports muscle growth and repair
  • Enhances brain function in certain conditions

How to Use Creatine?

Creatine is commonly consumed in the form of powder mixed with water or a protein shake. It is often taken as a loading dose for the first few days, followed by a maintenance dose.
